Batman faces the Joker, a criminal mastermind who wants to create chaos. The film explores themes of justice and heroism with thrilling action and an iconic performance by Heath Ledger.

A thief enters people’s dreams to steal secrets but is tasked with planting an idea in someone’s mind. The film is a mind-bending thriller that explores dreams, reality, and illusion.
